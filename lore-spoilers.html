Lore Spoilers
How to use:
1. Add your spoiler tag (e.g., "[SPOILER]") to the *start* of a World Info entry's content.
2. Write your plaintext spoiler after the tag.
3. Click out of the box (blur). The text will be hidden.
4. Click back into the box (focus) to reveal and edit.
The LLM will always see the original, un-hidden text.
